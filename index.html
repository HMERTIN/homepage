About

Bridging finance requirements and SAP depth

Since being introduced to SAP as a student in 1995, I've spent three decades building deep expertise across SAP Financials and Controlling — from the day-to-day core of Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Fixed Assets and the General Ledger, through Cost Center Accounting and Internal Orders in CO, to Financial Supply Chain Management. In November 2011 I stepped out on my own, founding an independent SAP consulting practice focused on complex, international finance transformations.

My work sits at the intersection of business and system: translating real-world requirements — tax law, treasury regulation, lease accounting — into robust SAP configuration, customizing and ABAP-supported solutions. I've contributed to more than 15 full-cycle and template rollouts across North and South America, Asia, Africa and Europe, alongside hands-on knowledge of LSMW, SAP-BI, Business Objects reporting and ABAP user exits/BADIs.

An effective communicator and integrator — bridging business requirements with the technical and functional depth of SAP.
